About Me

I'm Thavinsa Kariapperuma, a driven and multidisciplinary student currently pursuing a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science at the University of Westminster, UK, a BSc in Physical Science at the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, and a CIM Level 6 Postgraduate Diploma in Digital Marketing. My academic journey reflects a rare blend of technical depth and business acumen.

My technical foundation spans Python, Java, JavaScript, Flutter, SQL, HTML/CSS, and Git. I've applied these skills across meaningful projects, including MediGo — a smart inter-hospital ambulance system — and a responsive SDG6 awareness website. I've further deepened my Python expertise through LinkedIn Learning certifications in GUI development, data analysis, and advanced programming.

What sets me apart is my drive to bridge technology and business value. My background in digital marketing, combined with a strong interest in cloud technologies and cybersecurity, positions me well for pre-sales and client-facing roles. I'm passionate about translating complex technical solutions into clear, compelling value propositions that resonate with diverse audiences.

Beyond academics, I am a Toastmasters Gavel Club Chartered President, an ABRSM Grade 4 certified pianist, and an active member of the IIT Rotaract Club. I have coordinated volunteer events serving 50+ participants, demonstrating leadership, communication, and teamwork. I thrive at the intersection of people, technology, and purpose — always eager to learn and contribute.
